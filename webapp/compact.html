Glossary

Key biology and data terms

Quick definitions for the biology and data terms used throughout the dashboard.

ORF

Open reading frame: a sequence stretch that looks like a possible protein-coding region.

CpG

A cytosine followed by guanine on the same DNA strand, often important in gene regulation.

GC content

The percentage of bases that are G or C in a region.

Motif

A short recurring DNA pattern that may have biological meaning.

Repeat

A sequence that appears multiple times, including low-complexity runs and other recurring patterns.

Parquet

A columnar analytics file format used for the project’s S3 data lake outputs.

Athena

The AWS SQL engine used to query those S3 Parquet datasets.

Genomic window

A fixed-size chromosome slice used for regional summaries such as GC%, ORFs, and motif counts.
